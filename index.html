Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 11, 2024

Overview

This privacy policy covers all software projects and browser extensions developed by Adam Nagy. I am committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how data is handled in my applications.

LinkedIn Job Auto Apply Pro

Data Collection and Storage

  • Form responses for auto-filling job applications (stored locally)
  • Job application history to prevent duplicate applications
  • User preferences and settings

Data Storage Location

All data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. No data is transmitted to external servers or third parties.

Permissions

The extension requires access to:

  • linkedin.com domains (to automate job applications)
  • Chrome storage (to save your preferences and form data locally)

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your stored data (it's all in your local browser storage)
  • Delete your data (by clearing browser storage or uninstalling the extension)
  • Use the software without any data collection (though this may limit functionality)

Updates to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated occasionally. Significant changes will be announced through the extension's update notes.
